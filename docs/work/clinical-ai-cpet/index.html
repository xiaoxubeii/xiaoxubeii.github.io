CPET-World, CPET Co-Pilot, VOX CPET, and clinical agent systems for cardiopulmonary assessment.

What this is

Clinical AI work focused on CPET data, physiological signals, review workbenches, and decision-support systems for cardiopulmonary assessment. The goal is not autonomous diagnosis; it is safer reviewer-facing intelligence.

CPET-World

Research-use foundation model and adaptive digital twin workbench for cardiopulmonary exercise testing.

CPET Co-Pilot

Real-time CPET monitoring and prediction system with safety-aware review surfaces.

VOX CPET

VO2 peak prediction and real-time AT classification workflows for CPET model development.

Clinical agents

Report analysis, trend summaries, risk support, exercise prescription assistance, and clinical review loops.

System ingredients

  • multi-center processed CPET datasets and deterministic splits
  • masked reconstruction, endpoint heads, residual scoring, and uncertainty
  • browser workbenches that make model behavior visible to reviewers
  • clinical safety boundaries around risk support and decision assistance
